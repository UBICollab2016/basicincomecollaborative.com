We believe financial security is a human right, and cash might be its most effective and efficient guarantee.

In a world of immense wealth, no one should live in poverty, nor should the middle class be consigned to a future of permanent stagnation. Automation and globalization are changing the nature of work, and these shifts require that we reimagine our social safety net to guarantee economic opportunity for all.

A universal basic income is a bold idea with a long history. We are embarking on a two-year journey to study the basic income in our contemporary context: How would it work? Who would benefit? How would we pay for it? And what is the political path to making it a reality?

We support researchers, organizers, artists, and policymakers who think not only about the universal basic income, but broadly about the role of unconditional cash transfers in guaranteeing financial security for all.
